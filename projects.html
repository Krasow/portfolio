EXPERIENCE PROJECTS

clemsonrunningclub.com

Major update to the Clemson Running Club's website. Completely redesigned layout and new user features. Members can now login and sync their Strava (in order to track miles ran). A points redemption system was added in order to spend points earned from running. A blog feed has also been included to post important information, including upcoming events, for club members. A combination of Django, Jinja, Python, HTML, CSS, and Javascript was used to achieve this.
